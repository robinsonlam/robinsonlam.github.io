Getting There

Driving

Parking will be provided at Georges River 16ft Sailing Club, the ceremony will be located on the left after entering the car park, towards Peter Depena Reserve.

Georges River Sailing Club Courtesy Bus

An hourly courtesy bus will be available from Georges River 16ft Sailing Club for pick ups and drop offs (pick up is free but drop off is $3) within a 5 km radius, i.e. from Kogarah Station. The bus runs from 1 pm to 11 pm. Please call 0410 424 408 to make your own arrangements, or see https://georgesriversailingclub.com.au/ for more information.

Public Transport

There are various buses from nearby stations with stops nearby. Please see https://transportnsw.info/trip/ to organise your trip.
